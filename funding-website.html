01. Project Overview

Creating a game

Pestilence is a 2D tile-based action adventure game programmed entirely with P5JS. The player controls a knight-like character who must collect tokens and defeat enemies in order to cure a village of its plague. The player must explore several unique areas of the map, which include a monster-filled maze and the old village itself.
