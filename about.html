Hello, I'm

Brooke Zrymiak!

a versatile designer based in Metro Vancouver, who creates cohesive, user-focused solutions.

I'm a second year studying Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University, a multidisciplinary field where I create a variety of projects in all forms of design, including spatial, web, game, and UI/UX design.

My love for making things led me to study design, where I learned to create distinctive, clear, detail-oriented projects that emphasize creating unique experiences. My biggest inspirations are haute couture fashion, midcentury modern furniture, novelty souvenirs, and maximalist design practices. I love all things unconventional, statement, and unprecedented. More than anything, I am committed to designing with craftsmanship and character.

I'm a cat person, a clown enthusiast, and strong believer in luck. Ask me about my favourite thousand-paged French classics.
