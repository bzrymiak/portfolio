Hello, I'm

Brooke Zrymiak,

an interdisciplinary designer with a love for problem-solving, prototyping, and user-centric design.

I'm a third year student in Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University, a multidisciplinary field where I create a variety of projects in all forms of design. I'm extremely passionate about game design/development and web dev, which encouraged me to pursue a minor in Computer Science.

My personal inspirations are haute couture fashion, novelty souvenirs, and all things unconventional, memorable, and full of personality. More than anything, I am committed to designing with craftsmanship and character.
