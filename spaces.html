Project Overview

For SparkJam 2026, a 5-day design sprint hosted by SFU Surge, my team and I decide to build on discord following the prompt “add an creation-based feature to a non-creation software,” meaning that we would need to find a way to create an interesting and meaningful customization-based addition to Discord that simultaneously felt native to the software.

Our pitch video can be viewed on YouTube and our full prototype on Figma.
